Profile

About Me

PhD researcher focused on multimodal AI, cross-modal alignment, and robust generalization.

My doctoral work at the University of Electronic Science and Technology of China built a large-scale multimodal ReID benchmark spanning RGB, thermal infrared, infrared, and text, with methods for alignment and test-time adaptation.

In industry and public-sector collaborations, I have delivered 50+ applied AI projects for UNDP, I‑SAPS, LAS Pakistan, and companies including ESGnie, VikkAI, Lincware, BlueOcean, Staff, Cracked.AI, and Atomcamp.

I also lead professional training in Generative AI, Agentic AI, NLP, and MLOps for national and international audiences.
